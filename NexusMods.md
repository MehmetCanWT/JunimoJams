[size=5][b][color=#4D7C0F]🌿 The Story[/color][/b][/size]
You’re watering your crops as the sun sets over Pelican Town, and a perfect song starts playing. You want to know what it is. You [i]Alt+Tab[/i] to check, the cozy immersion breaks, you miss the sunset, and Pierre’s shop is closed by the time you tab back in. 

[b][color=#78350F]Never again.[/color][/b]

[b]Junimo Jams[/b] (formerly [i]Spotify Valley[/i]) is a premium, lightweight, and highly customizable in-game HUD that bridges the gap between your farm and your music. It dynamically fetches track details, high-res covers, and provides full media control—all integrated seamlessly into Stardew Valley’s gorgeous interface.

No matter if you use Spotify, YouTube Music, Apple Music, or high-fidelity local players like TIDAL—[b]Junimo Jams[/b] has you covered.

[size=5][b][color=#4D7C0F]✨ Premium Features[/color][/b][/size]
[list]
[*] [b]Real-Time Album Art Search[/b] — Automatically searches and retrieves high-resolution covers. Uses an advanced Jaccard similarity algorithm to ensure 99% matching accuracy even for generic/browser playing states.
[*] [b]Tactile HUD Controls[/b] — Prev, Play/Pause, and Next buttons rendered directly onto the HUD frame. Control your music without tabbing out.
[*] [b]Cozy Theming Engine[/b] — Comes with multiple handcrafted themes including the pastel [b]Overgrown Grass Flowery[/b] theme (adorned with delicate vines and flowers) and the classic [b]Stardew Valley Wood[/b] aesthetic.
[*] [b]Universal Character Map[/b] — Turkish, Cyrillic, and special international characters are automatically normalized, meaning no more broken fonts or weird boxes.
[*] [b]True Cross-Platform[/b] — Built to run flawlessly on Windows, macOS, and Linux (fully integrated with MPRIS2 desktop audio protocols).
[*] [b]Zero Performance Impact[/b] — Features a smart local caching system for album covers and redraws only when state changes. Your FPS remains completely untouched.
[/list]
